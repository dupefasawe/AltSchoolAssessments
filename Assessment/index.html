How to make Banana Bread

This classic treat is perfect for using up ripe bananas and filling your home with the delicious aroma of freshly baked goodness. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced baker, these simple steps will guide you to a moist, flavorful loaf that’s perfect for breakfast, snacks, or dessert. Before we get baking, here is a quick summary of our recipe details:

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 55-65 minutes
  • Servings: 8 slices
  • Difficulty: Easy

List of Ingredients

The following are the ingredients needed to make your delicious banana bread:

  • 3 medium ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of milk or buttermilk

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt

Optional Ingredients

  • 1/2 a cup of nuts
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate Chips
  • 1/4 cup of coconut shavings
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan with butter or line it with parchment paper for easy removal.
  2. Mash the ripe bananas in a large mixing bowl using a fork or potato masher. Stir in the melted butter until combined.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, the beaten egg, and vanilla extract until well-blended.
  4. Add the flour and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter, as this can result in a dense bread.
  5. Fold in any optional mix-ins like nuts or chocolate chips with a spatula.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 55 to 65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
  8. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing the bread and transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Helpful Tips:

  • Add chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans) for a crunchy surprise.
  • Throw in some chocolate chips for extra sweetness.
